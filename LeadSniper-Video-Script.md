## 💡 Pro-Tips for Recording and AI Voice Generation

1. **AI Voiceover Generation (ElevenLabs)**:
   * Go to ElevenLabs, choose a voice with a clean B2B tone (like "Adam" or "Marcus").
   * Copy the exact **VO** blocks above.
   * Generate the audio file and download it.
2. **Screen Recording (Windows Game Bar / Snipping Tool)**:
   * Press `Win + Alt + R` (Game Bar) to record your active browser window in full HD.
   * Or press `Win + Shift + R` (Snipping Tool) to record a custom crop area of your browser window (this hides browser tabs and bookmarks, making the video look extremely clean).
3. **Editing (Screen Studio / CapCut / Premiere)**:
   * Drop the recorded video and the ElevenLabs audio track into your editor.
   * Align the visual actions exactly to the voiceover cues.
   * Zoom in on key areas: the **3D Radar sidepanel**, the **Intent Score**, and the **RPA auto-populate action**.
   * Add a subtle background synthwave beat to tie it together!
